My Blog

3 years of being a student in Khon Kaen University.

July 12, 2025

Since I started studying at Khon Kaen University as a student in the Faculty of Engineering, majoring in Computer Engineering, I’ve been very happy. I’ve met many friends, and everyone is friendly.
I’ve enjoyed both studying and traveling with my group of friends — it has been a great life during my first year.

In my second year, the studies became more serious, so I didn’t get to travel as much, but I still found happiness in each day. On days when some classes ended in the evening, I enjoyed looking up at the sky — it helped me relax and feel less tired. After that,
I would go find something delicious to eat before heading back to my dorm.

In my third year, not much time has passed yet, but it’s already been an incredibly busy period. I have to visit various companies related to my field of study, and when I return, I also work part-time. Even though it’s just a little experience,
it’s been worthwhile. I hope this will turn out to be a good year.
